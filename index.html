About Graphene

GrapheneROM is a custom version of the Android Open Source Project that is designed and built from the ground up majorly with speed in mind. But while speed is the largest part, we don't leave out what we all love, the reason we choose Android: Customization and Features. Our goal is to create the fastest ROM possible, while using only the features and options that we feel are the most usefull, trimming down on all the copy and paste work and intimidating feature lists of other custom ROMs. Let's follow the standard Android Lollipop set. Let's keep it fast and simple.

Key Features

Active Display
Using the proximity sensor, your device will detect when it is in your pocket and not turn on the display when you recieve a notification. When you take your device out of your pocket, the display will automatically turn on to alert you of notifications without you having to do a thing. Thanks to ChameleonOS, Active dsplay has been modified to use less battery and a special notification screen to use the least battery as possible.
Redesigned Lockscreen Notifications
*Currently a WIP* Liked the custom lockscreen notifications feature available in a lot of kitkat ROM's, but not Lollipop's? Our redesign of the notifications allows them to be hidden with only an icon showing, giving you the same privacy you had before, as well as the less clustered and more sleek feel of the original feature, while incorporating the same material design look Lollipop brings.
